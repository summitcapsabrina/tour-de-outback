Registration History

Upload rider history, search registrants, and see the data by year.

Upload a CSV of past registrations. After you choose a file you'll confirm which column maps to which field before anything is saved — nothing uploads until you do. One row per registration (a rider who rode two years appears twice). Riders are matched to their account by email, so one column must be mapped to Email. Anyone who registered every year with no gaps is flagged grandfathered.

Every rider in this file is recorded under 2026, so that year shows on their profile. Upload one file per year to build each rider's history. For 2025 and later, also map a Date registered column to plot them on the progress chart — earlier years usually won't have exact dates, and that's fine. To track who showed up, add a Checked in? column — put any mark (an X, a ✓, or “yes”) next to riders who checked in and leave it blank for the rest; re-uploading with that column is the authoritative record for the year.

Match each column to a field in your list. Columns set to Ignore (do not import) are skipped. Here are the first 5 lines to help:

Sync

Click Sync shop with Printify after any change in Printify — adding a new product, or editing an existing one (price, description, colors, photos). It does two things: finishes publishing any brand-new product (Printify's custom stores leave new products stuck "publishing"), and refreshes the storefront so your edits appear on /shop/ right away (give the page a refresh; allow up to a minute).

Discounts & Credits

Set up the discounts and account credits that will apply at registration checkout. This is built ahead of the in-house registration flow, so it's ready the day registration opens — nothing here affects riders until then.

Registration discount codes

A rider enters a code at checkout. Free registration takes the whole fee off; you can also give a percentage or a dollar amount off. Set Max uses to limit how many times a code can be redeemed (use 1 for a one-time free registration, or leave it blank for unlimited). Codes are not case-sensitive.

Loading codes…

Account credits

Tie a credit to an email address — including riders who haven't made an account yet (for example, credit carried over from the old system). Enter the dollar amount they should be credited. When someone checks out with that same email, the credit comes off their registration fee automatically. Credit is applied after any discount code, and anything left over stays on the account for next time. Enter more for the same email to add to their credit; to correct a mistake, remove the credit and re-enter it.
