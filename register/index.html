What's Included

Every registration includes fully supported aid stations along your route, event swag, and access to Camp Tour de Outback at the Lake County Fairgrounds.

On Friday evening, join the warm-up ride, pizza party with live music, beer garden, and raffle drawing. On Saturday after your ride, refuel at the post-ride lunch.

All proceeds benefit Lake County Search and Rescue.
