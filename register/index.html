Save by Registering Early

The earlier you register, the more you save. Early Bird pricing is available through January 30. General registration runs through May 31. After that, late registration rates apply through ride start.

Register Now on BikeReg

What's Included

Every registration includes fully supported aid stations along your route, a post-ride festival with food, drinks, and live music, event swag, and a finisher reward.

You'll also get access to Camp Tour de Outback at the Lake County Fairgrounds, the pre-ride social on Friday evening, and the awards ceremony on Saturday afternoon.

All proceeds benefit Lake County Search and Rescue.
