🚿 Showers

At the fairgrounds: coin-operated showers cost $3.50 per shower — in quarters. Plan ahead and bring a roll if you can.

Free showers at the high school: Lakeview High School is opening its locker rooms to riders. Look for signage from the fairgrounds.

⛺ Camping at the Fairgrounds

RV / Tent Dry Camping: $15 per night

RV Hookup Camping: $30 per night — SOLD OUT

Need to add camping to your registration? Head back to BikeReg, select Camping Options (skip the ride selection), use the email you originally registered with, and check out. Staying extra nights outside the listed dates? Pick “Additional Day” from the drop-down for each one — we'll reach out to confirm.

Dry camping? Bring portable chargers for your devices.

Don't Forget

Pack bug spray and sunscreen. The high desert sun is no joke at 4,800 ft, and the bugs are along for the ride.
