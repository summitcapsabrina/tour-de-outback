A Huge Lake County Accomplishment

By Dave Tucker

A few miles into the road routes this year, you'll roll past something brand-new in downtown Lakeview: the Wheels-In-Motion Skatepark, opened September 13, 2025. What used to be a cracked, abandoned tennis court is now a 6,000-square-foot Dreamland-designed concrete masterpiece — and the largest capital project in Rotary District 5110's history.

It was built by a town of about 2,300 people who refused to let go of an idea. We wrote up the whole story.

As you ride by, take a look. You're not just seeing a skatepark. You're seeing what Lakeview can do.
