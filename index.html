Camp Tour de Outback

This year, we're excited to announce Camp Tour de Outback will be hosted at the Lake County Fairgrounds in Lakeview, Oregon!

Enjoy RV and tent dry camping right at the event venue. With wide open spaces, small-town hospitality, and stunning high-desert scenery, Lakeview is the perfect basecamp for your adventure.
