Camping at the Fairgrounds

Camp Tour de Outback is hosted at the Lake County Fairgrounds in Lakeview, Oregon. Camping is available for both RVs and tents.

RV / Tent Dry Camping — $15/night. Each reservation is for 1 tent, maximum 2 people per tent. Please bring portable chargers for your devices.

RV Hookup Camping — $30/night.

If you didn't select camping during registration, you can go back and add it. Please do not select a ride — scroll past and select from the Camping Options, enter the email you used to register, then checkout.
