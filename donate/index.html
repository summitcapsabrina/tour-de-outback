Support Lake County Search and Rescue

Oregon Tour de Outback exists to celebrate the wide-open beauty of Lake County — and to give back to the volunteers who keep this country safe. 100% of every donation supports Lake County Search and Rescue, the all-volunteer team that responds to lost hikers, injured riders, and emergencies across nearly 8,300 square miles of high desert.

You don't need to ride to be part of this event. A donation, large or small, fuels training, gear, and the rapid response that saves lives.
