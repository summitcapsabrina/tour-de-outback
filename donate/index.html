Support Oregon Tour de Outback

Your gift supports Oregon Tour de Outback — and through the event, our long-time partners at Lake County Search and Rescue, the all-volunteer team that responds to lost hikers, injured riders, and emergencies across nearly 8,300 square miles of high desert.

You don't need to ride to be part of this event. A contribution, large or small, helps fund the rides, the riders' safety net, and the community partnerships that make this event possible.
